Much Adieu About Spinning

In the beginning, there was Borpa, spinning every which way. As he span, he began rapidly approaching terminal spin velocity. Then, something strange happened...

The primal monke, clad in brown fur and opposable thumbs, appeared in the thick of night. He knew not what the Borpa sought with his spinning, but it mattered not: Monke approached with reckless abandon, determined to save the now-dizzy Borpa.
